We are Leading, Exporter & Wholesaler of Dust Bags, Cloth Dust Bags, Shopping Bags, Non Woven Bags and Garment Bags in India

Bushr's originated in 2008 for manufacturing various type of Eco-friendly jute bags and cotton bags like Beach Bags, Drawstring Bags, Grow Bags, Non-Woven Bags, Official Bags, Promotional Bags, Shopping Bags, Toy Bags, Wine Bags and much more as per every customer need. Taking pride in our work, we started our export business from the year 2008.

With over 16 years of manufacturing operations, we supply Jute, Cotton, and Non-Woven bags to many jute bag exporters in India and world-wide through many corporate sectors. Our products are exported to the overseas markets of USA, UK, UAE, Germany, United Kingdom, Canada, Spain, France, Netherlands,Thailand, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, Denmark, Singapore, Russia, South Africa, Italy & Japan and many other countries across the Globe

Our rate is much lower than any other export house because our overhead expenditure is marginal as the entire manufacturing process are being carried on under one roof.

Infrastructure

It has a manufacturing unit which is equipped with the state-of-the-art technology outskirts of Kolkata (5 KM from NSCBI Airport, Dum Dum), West Bengal, India. Our Skilled designers and diligent employees manufacturing modern and trendy export quality jute bags with the help of latest machines and tools. The factory is equipped with state-of-the-art Cutting, printing, stitching, checking, packing under one roof for the manufacture of jute bags.

Quality

We procure premium quality raw materials from renowned and trustworthy suppliers of the industry, and test those thoroughly before using it in our manufacturing unit. Our experienced Quality Control Team conducts strict quality checking of each product on the parameters of as strength, design, pattern,color, size, etc. Once all the parameters are fully met with, then only are the products marked as Finished & Packaged. We manufacture and export best quality jute bags and cotton bags in India.

We check each product very carefully to maintain the product quality. No one can beat our stitching quality.

Social responsibility

We promote responsible farming practices, minimize waste generation, and invest in eco-friendly production processes.
