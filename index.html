    Why jute?

    Jute products present a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to conventional materials. Derived from the jute plant, which is biodegradable and renewable, these products boast low environmental impact throughout their lifecycle. Furthermore, jute is known for its durability and strength, making it ideal for a wide range of applications such as packaging, textiles, and home decor.

    Right from the first steps of cultivation, jute gives back more than it takes. A hectare of jute consumes 15 tonnes of carbon dioxide and releases 11 tonnes of oxygen in 120 days of growth.

    By choosing jute products, consumers contribute to the reduction of plastic usage and support ethical and fair-trade practices often associated with jute cultivation and manufacturing.

    Why Cotton?

    Cotton bags are reusable and durable, reducing the need for single-use plastics and minimizing environmental waste. They are also biodegradable, which means they break down naturally over time, posing less harm to ecosystems.

    Cotton bags can be washed and reused multiple times, making them a sustainable choice for shoppers. Their sturdy construction allows them to carry heavy loads, making them suitable for groceries, books, and other items.
